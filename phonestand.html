Phone Stand TinkerCad

In trimester 3, we worked on 3D printing designs using TinkerCad. I deisgned a phone stand by adding other unique creations such as the star fish and small characters. However, if I had more time, I would've used less details on my phone stand because it would be difficult to 3D print due to the melting plastic. The skills I was able to learn from this was to know when to stop because too much of something is not always a good thing. Overall, I was able to express my creativity though this project!

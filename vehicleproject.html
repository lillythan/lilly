While I'm more familiar with using TinkerCad, the objective of this project was to create a vehicle that would use Arudino parts such as LED lights, DC motors, bread board...etc. During the process of constructing the car, it required critical thinking because I had to incorporate real life Arduino parts in order for the car to be functional. Therefore, I faced a lot of trial and error and had to work around that. The most challenging part of this project was the measurements and holes for the Arduino parts. If I had more time, I would have made a simple design on the car that specializes for the purpose of Arduino parts. I would've also designed the car in a different way to make my vehicle printable.
